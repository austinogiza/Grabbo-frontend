DR. KATE O. ONOJA, (MBBS, FWACS, FMCOG).
Dr. Onoja is a Consultant Obstertrics & Gynaecology and a fertility specialist. She is a 2005 graduate of the College of Medicine, University of Jos.
As a student, she had distinction in Physiology and Biochemistry.

She later won an award as the best graduating medical student. She did her housemanship in Jos University Teaching Hospital (JUTH) in 2006. Thereafter, she worked as a medical Officer in Dalhatu Araf Specialist hospital, Lafia.

She later had her residency (specialist) training in Obstetrics and Gynaecology in Jos University between 2007 and 2012. She finished in record time.
Before setting up Grabbo Fertility clinic and diagnostic centre, she worked in Nisa Premiere Hospital, Abuja between 2012 and 2013 as a consultant Obstetrician and Gynaecologist. She also worked in Kelina Hospital, Abuja, as HOD of Obstetrics and Gynaecology.
